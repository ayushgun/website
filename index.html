Hi, I'm Ayush.

I'm a computer science undergraduate at the Georgia Institute of Technology concentrating in machine learning and system architecture.

Recently, I was a software engineering intern on a low latency systematic trading desk at Citadel Securities, where I worked on building systems for market making.

As an undergraduate researcher at the High Performance Architecture Lab at Georgia Tech, I explored high-throughput GPU allocation policies and in-storage GPU architectures in partnership with Samsung Research.

Before that, I was an undergraduate researcher at the Even-Tov group of the University of California, Berkeley, where I built low-touch sentiment analysis pipelines for disclosure indicator classification.

I am particularly interested in systems programming, high performance computing for ML, and heterogeneous computing. If you'd like to read my writings on these topics, see my blog.
